Twenty-four rulers of imagined kingdoms — each one a world built from scratch.
Sovereign NFT Studio needed characters that felt genuinely mythological — not derivative, not formulaic. Each of the 24 sovereigns was developed with a full backstory, visual language, and symbolic system. The brief: make collectors feel they've discovered something ancient that was always waiting for them.
Three of 24 sovereigns — The Verdant, The Obsidian, The Celestial
Process

Building Mythology from Nothing

World Architecture
Before a single character was sketched, the world was built. A taxonomy of 6 kingdoms, each with distinct elemental allegiance, architectural motif, and symbolic language. The characters would grow from this — not be imposed upon it.
Character Development
Each sovereign was developed individually: backstory, personality, visual archetype, and symbolic vocabulary. Costume design, crown construction, heraldic systems, companion iconography. 24 complete characters over 10 weeks.
Collectible Release
The series launched in three waves of 8, with reveal campaigns built around mystery and exclusivity. Purchasers received the character art plus a full mythological dossier. All 24 sold within 72 hours of final wave release.
"The character series exceeded every brief we gave. There's a depth of world-building in each piece that our collectors genuinely respond to. We sold out in 72 hours."
Marcus Thorn
Founder, Sovereign NFT Studio
