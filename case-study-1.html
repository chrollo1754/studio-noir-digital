A fragrance house that wanted its visual world to feel as intoxicating as the scent itself.
Maison Obscura came to Studio Noir with a single brief: make it feel like falling into something beautiful and irreversible. The result was a complete brand visual system — illustration, packaging direction, and campaign imagery — built around the mythology of depth, shadow, and surrender.

Process

From Brief to Mythology

Discovery & Direction
Three rounds of mood-board exploration, distilling the brand's core emotional territory: depth, shadow, a controlled elegance. We settled on fragrance mythology — the idea that scent carries memory, and memory carries you somewhere you can't return from.
Illustration System
Built a modular botanical and architectural illustration language — elements that could live on bottles, campaign pages, and social. Every element carries the weight of something aged, chosen deliberately rather than generated. Hand-finished linework throughout.
Campaign Application
Extended the system across launch campaign imagery, digital placements, and retail packaging. The visual world became self-referential — each touchpoint felt like a chapter of the same story, with the fragrance as its unreliable narrator.
